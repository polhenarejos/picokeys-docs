# FAQ

## Is PicoKeyApp open source?
The application may be distributed as a binary. Some firmware components and related tools may be available as open source depending on the project.

## Why does PicoKeyApp require a license?
Licensing may be used to enable advanced features and to support ongoing development.

## Can I use PicoKeyApp offline?
Depending on the build, most core functionality works offline. Some update mechanisms may require internet access.
